Brands

Brand cards with a short “personality” description and an interesting fact.

Zara

A fast-fashion brand known for quickly turning runway trends into affordable everyday clothing. New collections appear very frequently.

Fact: Zara is famous for its short production cycle — “seen on the runway, available in stores”.

Nike

A mix of sport and lifestyle. Known for innovation in footwear, strong branding, and cultural influence through sports and collaborations.

Fact: Nike played a major role in making sneakers part of everyday fashion.

Adidas

Strong sports heritage combined with streetwear culture. Iconic three stripes, classic sneaker silhouettes, and a deep connection to football and street style.

Fact: Adidas is especially strong in retro aesthetics and collaborations.

H&M

Affordable fashion with frequent collection updates and popular designer collaborations. A good choice for basics and current trends.

Fact: H&M regularly releases limited designer collections.

Uniqlo

Functional minimalism focused on quality basics, clean design, and innovative fabrics. Perfect for building a capsule wardrobe.

Fact: Uniqlo is known for its HeatTech and AIRism fabric technologies.

Levi’s

Denim heritage brand where jeans became a cultural symbol. Iconic fits, jackets, and timeless styles.

Fact: Levi’s is one of the most recognizable denim brands in the world.
Tip: choose brands based on your goal - Uniqlo for a clean capsule wardrobe, Adidas/Nike for street and sport, Zara/H&M for fast-changing trends.
